Teaching

Arizona State University

I teach two courses to the FT-MBA students at ASU, typically in the Fall.

  • SCM 551: Operations Planning and Execution (MBA Elective, MS-SCM)[syllabus]

    • The course is centered around operational decision-making in environments with uncertainty. The course contains a few simulations/experiments. Students are encouraged to build a decision support tool that aids them in decision-making.
    • The course contains mild use of Python, and is taught via separate tutorials. I used this reference book in the past.

  • SCM 501/502: Operations and Supply Chain Management (FT MBA, MiM Core) [syllabus]

    • I plan to maintain an independent repository of past content – this is work-in-progress. Students may use this at their own discretion.
